Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how MemRush ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects user information, including your rights regarding account deletion.

Google Play Compliance: This policy is designed to meet the requirements of the Google Play Data Safety section.

1. Information We Collect

In order to provide a personalized gameplay experience and account security, we collect the following data:

  • Personal Information: E-mail address and Username provided during registration.
  • Game Progress: Scores, levels reached, XP, missions completed, and daily reward history (stored via Firebase Firestore).
  • Device Data: Unique User IDs (UID), approximate location (Country code), and device identifiers for analytics.

2. How We Use Information

  • To manage your game account and synchronize progress across devices.
  • To display your ranking in the Global Leaderboard.
  • To provide technical support and fix technical issues.
  • To show advertisements via Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted Google services to operate MemRush:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication and cloud data storage.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements.
  • Firebase Analytics: To understand app usage and improve features.

4. Account and Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and provide two ways to delete your account and all associated data:

A) In-App Deletion: You can delete your account directly through the Settings menu within the app. This action is immediate and permanent.

B) Email Request: You can request account deletion by sending an e-mail to burkanemretokman@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

What happens when you delete your account?

  • Your e-mail address and password will be removed from Firebase Authentication.
  • Your username, high scores, levels, and all game progress will be permanently deleted from our Firestore database.
  • Your entry in the Global Leaderboard will be removed.
  • Data is typically wiped from our active systems within 7 days.

5. Data Retention

We store your data as long as your account is active. If your account remains inactive for more than 24 months, we reserve the right to delete your data to protect your privacy and reduce data storage.

6. Security

All data is transferred using secure HTTPS/TLS encryption. We rely on Google's industry-leading security infrastructure to keep your information safe.

7. Children’s Privacy

MemRush does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have accidentally collected such data, please contact us for immediate removal.
