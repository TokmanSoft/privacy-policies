Privacy Policy – MemRush

Last Updated: May 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how MemRush (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects user data in compliance with Google Play Developer Program Policies.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and security)
  • Username

1.2 App Activity

  • Game progress (levels, XP, scores, missions)
  • Daily rewards and leaderboard data

1.3 Device Identifiers

  • Firebase UID, device model, and approximate country-level location.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and database management, and Google AdMob for advertisements. These services handle data securely according to Google’s global standards.

3. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active. Accounts inactive for more than 24 months may be removed to protect user privacy.

4. Account and Data Deletion

In-App Deletion (Recommended):
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the app. Navigate to Settings and tap on DELETE ACCOUNT. This action is immediate and will permanently remove your profile, scores, and progress from our servers.

Manual Request:
Alternatively, you may request account deletion by emailing burkanemretokman@gmail.com from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 7 days.

5. Security

All data transfers are encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols to ensure your information remains private and secure.
